- Platelets: Number of platelets, a type of blood cell involved in clotting, in the blood (10^3/μL)
- Prothrombin: Level of prothrombin, a protein involved in blood clotting, that can indicate liver damage (seconds)
- Stage: Stage of liver cirrhosis (1-4, with higher numbers indicating more advanced disease)
